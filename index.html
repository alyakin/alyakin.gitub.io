Class Descriptions

ROWING
  • Row 50 50 minutes of nothing but rowing. Speed, endurance, and strength will power you through this cardio based workout.

  • Row Interval Rowing plus full body strength in a fun, heart pumping, interval format. Get the best of both worlds!

  • Row Power Challenge yourself and max out by tracking your power during each rowing round. Friendly competition with others in class for an extra power boost. Competition is based off different components including meters, time, and stroke/second.
YOGA
  • Hot Vinyasa Gain strength, flexibility, and relaxataion as we flow through poses. Vinyasa yoga but in a heated room! (Room heated to about 90 degrees)

  • Vinyasa Yoga Find clarity and focus using breath to move you from asana to asana creating a flowing practice. (Room heated to 75 -80 Degrees)

  • Power Vinyasa A brisk, invigorating vinyasa flow designed to make you sweat while moving through a variety of asanas. You'll leave feeling powerful yet relax! (Room heated to 80 – 85 degrees)

  • Recovery Flow A slower paced vinyasa style class to help recover your body and relax your mind. Recovery yoga is a perfect way to unwind after a long day. (Room heated to 80 – 85 degrees)
TRX/HYBRID
  • TRX Full body strength and cardio utilizing the TRX Suspension Trainer - Suspension Training bodyweight exercise develops strength, balance, flexibility and core stability simultaneously. It requires the use of the TRX Suspension Trainer, a highly portable performance training tool that leverages gravity and the user's body weight to complete hundreds of exercises

  • Row and Flow 30 minute high intensity Rowing followed by 45 minutes of flexibility enhancing yoga
